The Econo-Shock-Transfer-O-Meter

Inspired by the saying "If America sneezes, the world catches a cold" - how will various shocks transfer around the world economy, based on differing assumptions?

1. Set the shock transmission assumptions

These assumptions are used to analyse how shocks pass between countries and sectors.

Please select:

  • Input threshold x% - a supply shock passes from one sector to another sector, if the shocked sector is at least x% of the total inputs needed to produce the other sector's product.
  • Imported amount threshold y% - a supply shock passes from one country's exports of a product to another country, if the exports are at least y% of the total amount of the product imported.
  • Critical industry threshold z% - a country is treated as experiencing a shock if any producer is experiencing a shock, and that producer makes at z% of national output.
